[02] features // v1, windows 10 / 11

Two display modes

A horizontal key feed, or an on-screen keyboard layout. Switch any time from the tray.

Always on top

The overlay is transparent, click-through, and stays above other windows. It does not show up in the taskbar.

OBS friendly

Visible to OBS and standard screen capture on Windows, so it shows up in your stream or recording.

Tray controlled

Lives in the system tray. Right-click for settings, position, and to quit. No window to manage.

Quiet when idle

The native overlay does almost nothing when you are not typing. No background animation loop burning your CPU.
